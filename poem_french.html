Time and Light
Anise Koltz
We survive in this world
stricken with light
in order to pay our part of the dream
that will survive us
The hither-thither flight
of a butterfly
can change the climate
of a continent
Each dawn
is a promise of eternity

Each sunset
its flamboyant cancellation
We have left the future behind us
like an abolished religion

We are astray
in the bible of time
The sea is rising up along the stairs
knocking at my door
Lost in its own immensity
it angles for hospitality
And lies down under my bed
The stones
deprived of any pathway
are breathless
in immobility
Portraits
exhausted as gardens are
in autumn
husband their withered past
The earth will only turn
for a few seconds

How can I take my place in history
flowing along beside my blood
We left the ocean
billions of years ago
The land has been promised us
and earth will come out of our mouths
Lost in space
eternity turns back
to the glacial tournera

Keeps watch over our petrified bodies
sites abandoned by time
