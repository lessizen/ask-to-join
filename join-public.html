the library

time to take a walk!
provided by your taxes
internet awaits

1, 2, 14 blocks
grateful for public spaces
walk thru metal gates

find those glowing screens
past fiction and Charles Dickens,
poems, biographies

audiobooks, CD's,
periodicals, vinyls
from the seventies

alas, you've made it
portals to online places
it's time to dial up

ignore the stranger
sitting next to you, staring
at you and your screen

lastly, don't forget
privacy is a blessing
but it's never free
